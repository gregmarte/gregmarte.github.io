Building Value

It's day 1 at a new company.
How are you going to help the organization reach their objectives?


So here's what an organziation wants:
  1. Business Value - in whatever they choose to count
  2. Business Agility - adaptibility to market changes
  3. Predictability - confidence in hitting your planned outcomes
  4. Visibility - show how you got there

This is an prioritized list.
We'll choose Business Value over Predictability every time.
