Building Value

It's day 1 at a new company.

What an organziation wants:

  1. Business Value - in whatever they choose to count (money, fame, fortune)
  2. Business Agility - adaptibility to market changes
  3. Predictability - confidence in hitting your planned outcomes
  4. Visibility - show how you got there

This is an prioritized list.
Which may mean choosing Business Value over Predictability.

However, ease of implementation is in reverse.

So as a new hire, I'm going to ask you to focus on Visiblity,
and once mastered, then we will talk about Predictability.
